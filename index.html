Join Us!


Savannah Foundation Trust [SFT] is a Malawian environmental non-governmental organization which was conceived in 2019 to help address numerous environmental challenges particularly in the remote parts of the country. As an organization, we attained full legal status in September 2020 under the Non-Governmental Organizations Act of 2005 and then got certified as The Registered Trustee of Savannah Foundation Trust with Certificate Number TR/INC: 8560. The organization is also a bonafide member of the Council of Non-governmental Organizations of Malawi (CONGOMA) with registration number C1454/2020.

T - Transformative Partnerships

We believe in deep, collaborative relationships. We don't work for communities but with them, transforming both the landscape and the dynamics of development to be locally-led and empowering.

H - Holistic Restoration

Our approach is integrated. We restore entire ecosystems—forests, soil, and water—to ensure environmental health is the foundation for sustainable livelihoods, resilient communities, and economic well-being.

R - Resilient Communities

We empower people to become the architects of their own future. By building capacity, fostering economic independence, and strengthening social bonds, we help communities withstand environmental and economic shocks.

I - Inclusive Empowerment

We are committed to equity and justice. We actively ensure that all community members, especially women, indigenous groups, and the most vulnerable, have an equal voice, opportunity, and share in the benefits of our work.

V - Verifiable Impact

We are stewards of trust and transparency. We are committed to measurable, evidence-based results in both ecological restoration and poverty reduction, ensuring accountability to our communities, partners, and supporters.

E - Environmental Stewardship

Our work is rooted in a profound respect for nature. We galvanize action to protect, restore, and sustainably manage natural resources, recognizing that a healthy planet is essential for all life to truly thrive.
