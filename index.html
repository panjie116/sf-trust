About Us

Leading Environmental Change in Malawi

Savannah Foundation Trust (SFT) is a pioneering Malawian environmental NGO, established in 2019 to tackle critical environmental challenges in remote communities.

Our Mission

To empower communities to become custodians of the environment through forest restoration, biodiversity conservation, and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Vision

A Malawi where nature and communities thrive together in harmony, fostering a green and prosperous future for generations to come.

Our Mission

Strategic Objectives

Driving meaningful change through focused initiatives

Environmental Education

Create a nature-friendly public through comprehensive environmental education and awareness programs.

Research & Knowledge

Advance the study and knowledge of Malawi's Natural History through evidence-based research.

Conservation

Conserve priority species, sites, and habitats in remote and vulnerable areas.

Policy Advocacy

Advocate for policies favorable to rural development and natural resource management.

Community Health

Promote research on community and environmental health for holistic rural development.

Youth Engagement

Engage youth in agro-forestry, entrepreneurship, and sustainable natural resource development.

Core Values

T.H.R.I.V.E Framework

The principles that guide our every action

T

Transformative Partnerships

We work with communities, government, and stakeholders to create lasting change through collaborative efforts.

H

Holistic Restoration

We restore forests, soil, and ecosystems while addressing the interconnected needs of communities.

R

Resilient Communities

We build capacity and strengthen local governance to create communities that can adapt and thrive.

I

Inclusive Empowerment

Women, youth, and marginalized groups are at the center of our development approach.

V

Verifiable Impact

We are committed to transparency, accountability, and measurable outcomes in all our work.

E

Environmental Stewardship

We inspire action to protect and restore natural resources for current and future generations.
